Tortilla

Description

Tortilla Española is a potato and onion omelette. It's a classic tapas dish, prepared all over Spain. This version also includes roasted red peppers and Spanish Serrano ham.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 onions, sliced into rings
  • 1 roasted red pepper, drained and cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh Italian parsley

Steps

  1. Heat 1/2 cup olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat
  2. Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a separate skillet over medium heat.
  3. Whisk eggs in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in cooled onions, roasted peppers, and serrano ham.
  4. Heat the large skillet with reserved oil over low heat.
  5. Slide the tortilla onto a serving plate and allow to cool to room temperature. Cut into six wedges and sprinkle with parsley to serve.
